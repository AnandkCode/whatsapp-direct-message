Privacy Policy

Introduction

At WhatsApp Direct Message, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our service.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information, including phone numbers or messages. Our service operates entirely on your device, generating WhatsApp chat links without transmitting any data to our servers.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use your data for any purpose. The phone numbers you enter are used solely to generate the WhatsApp chat link on your device.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your information. By not collecting or storing any personal data, we eliminate the risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our service.

Third-Party Services

Our service uses WhatsApp's official Click to Chat feature. We encourage you to review WhatsApp's privacy policy to understand how they handle your data when you use their service.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
